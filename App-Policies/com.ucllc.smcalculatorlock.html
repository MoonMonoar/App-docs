Privacy Policy for SM Calculator Lock

Welcome to SM Calculator Lock! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our application.

Data Collection and Processing:

SM Calculator Lock is designed to be a secure file lock application. We do not collect or process any personal information from our users. Your privacy is paramount, and we are committed to ensuring that your personal information remains safe and secure.

Permissions:

To provide our core functionality, SM Calculator Lock requires the following permissions on your device:

  1. All files permission: This permission is necessary for SM Calculator Lock to manage files effectively.
  2. Access to Photo, Video, and Audio files: These permissions are required to query the Media manager for locking purposes. They are used solely for managing files on your device.
  3. Ad display over other apps permission: This permission allows SM Calculator Lock to display advertisements over other applications.
  4. User actions monitoring: This permission is optional and enables SM Calculator Lock to monitor user actions for the purpose of enhancing app lock functionality. It allows the app to detect and respond to user actions that may trigger the app lock feature.
